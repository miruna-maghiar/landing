WHO WE ARE

The HEC Investment Club is an association dedicated to promoting the investment management universe at HEC Paris, one of the leading business schools in Europe.

Learning by doing is at the core of the philosophy of the club, which manages a real fund invested in Eurozone small and mid cap stocks, aiming at outperforming the MSCI Europe Small Cap index. Our 45 active members, split into 11 sector teams, perform thourough strategic and financial analysis and pitch their investment ideas during Investment Committees.

The club aims at reinforcing the ties between the buy-side industry (asset managers, hedge funds...) and the HEC community. ODDO BHF Asset Management, one the leading asset managers in Europe with â‚¬45bn under management, is the corporate partner of the HEC Investment Club.

The HEC Investment Club aims at doing good by doing well and gives 100% of its capital gains to the HEC Foundation, in order to finance scholarships for future generations of HEC students.
